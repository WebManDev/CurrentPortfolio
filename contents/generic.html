About Me

I'm Andy Luu, a dedicated student at Swarthmore College, majoring in Computer Science and Psychology. My academic pursuits have given me a solid foundation in areas such as Data Structures & Algorithms, Computer Systems, and Linear Algebra. My passion for technology is evident through my varied internship experiences, where I've honed my skills in software development, web development, and network technologies. Whether I'm creating interactive maps for the Princeton Eviction Lab or optimizing databases and enhancing user interfaces at the Science Mentorship Institute, I strive to leverage technology to solve real-world problems efficiently and effectively.

Beyond my formal education and professional experiences, I am deeply interested in the practical applications of computer science to drive innovation and societal impact. My project work, such as developing a Stock Market Predictor using Streamlit and Prophet, showcases my enthusiasm for data-driven decision-making and user-centric design. As an active member of the Google Student Developer Club, I enjoy collaborating with fellow tech enthusiasts to explore emerging technologies and tackle complex challenges. My journey in the tech world is driven by a continuous desire to learn, innovate, and make a positive difference through the power of technology.
